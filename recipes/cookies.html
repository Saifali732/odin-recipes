Description

This is the best chocolate chip cookie recipe that I have used. The recipe is very straight forward and easy to make. They're crispy and chewy and overall really good.

The recipe will make 36 cookies and take a total of 30 minutes to make

here is a link to the actual recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup softened, salted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chipes

Instructions:

  1. preheat oven to 365 degrees Farenheit and line pan with parchment paper
  2. mix flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder in a bowl
  3. cream together butter and sugars until combined
  4. beat eggs and vanilla until its fluffy
  5. mix dry Ingredients into the mixture until combined
  6. add the chocolate chips and mix well
  7. roll 2 TBS of dough and place in baking sheet spaced out evenly
  8. bake in oven for 8-10 minutes until barely brown
  9. take out and wait 2 minutes to cool before putting onto a cooling rack
